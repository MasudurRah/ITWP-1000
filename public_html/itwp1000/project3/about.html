RULES / CATEGORIES

Rules


Welcome to our photography website! We encourage all users to submit their captivating pictures and share their creativity with the community. To ensure a positive and fair experience for everyone, please take note of the following submission rules:

  1. 1. Keep pictures appropriate and family-friendly: We value a respectful and inclusive environment. Please refrain from submitting any content that is offensive, explicit, or violates any legal or ethical guidelines.
  2. 2. Voting is limited to one per hour per category: To maintain fairness in the voting process, each user is allowed to cast one vote per hour in each category. This ensures equal opportunities for all submitted photographs.
  3. 3. Limit of three pictures per day per category: We appreciate your enthusiasm, but to showcase a diverse range of talent, we kindly request that you submit a maximum of three pictures per day in each category. If you exceed this limit, only the first three submissions will be considered, and any subsequent pictures will be overridden.

Categories


Welcome to our photography website! We are thrilled to showcase the talent and creativity of our community. To keep things fresh and exciting, we accept submissions in three categories, which rotate bi-weekly. If you have any suggestions for future categories, please send us an email at our company email, located in the footer.

  1. 1. Majestic Mountains: Immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring world of mountains with the "Majestic Mountains" category. Capture the towering peaks, rugged cliffs, and sweeping valleys that define these majestic natural wonders. Showcase the diverse landscapes and unique geological formations found in mountain ranges around the globe. From the snow-capped summits to the lush green slopes, let your photographs encapsulate the grandeur and beauty of these majestic mountains.
  2. 2. Serene Sunsets: Indulge in the captivating beauty of "Serene Sunsets" and explore the enchanting moments when the sun paints the sky with vibrant hues. Capture the warm, golden light as it casts a serene glow over landscapes, seascapes, and cityscapes. From tranquil beaches to silhouetted skylines, let your photographs transport viewers to these magical moments, evoking a sense of peace, tranquility, and natural beauty..
  3. 3. Enchanting Waterfalls: Dive into the world of "Enchanting Waterfalls" and witness the power and grace of cascading water. Capture the dynamic flow, the misty spray, and the mesmerizing beauty of these natural wonders. From mighty cascades plummeting into deep gorges to delicate waterfalls gently trickling down moss-covered rocks, showcase the diversity of waterfalls and their surroundings. Let your photographs convey the ethereal and captivating nature of these enchanting waterfalls.

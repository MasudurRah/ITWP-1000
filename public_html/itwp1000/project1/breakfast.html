Bacon, Egg and Cheese

The bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich is a classic breakfast favorite. To make this delicious treat, start by heating a skillet over medium heat and adding a small amount of unsalted butter. Place the kaiser roll, bagel, or toasted and buttered bread in the skillet to lightly toast. In the same skillet, cook the halved slices of bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon and set it aside. In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork and season with kosher salt and black pepper. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and scramble them until fully cooked. Assemble the sandwich by placing the scrambled eggs, bacon slices, and American cheese between the toasted bread. For added flavor, you can also add ketchup and/or hot sauce to complete this mouthwatering bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich.


Ingredients

Unsalted butter, as needed
1 kaiser roll, split (or 1 bagel, split, or 2 slices of bread, toasted and buttered)
3 slices beef, pork or turkey bacon (not thick-cut), halved crosswise
2 large eggs, beaten with a fork
Kosher salt and black pepper
2 slices American cheese
Ketchup and/or hot sauce

Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is a simple and nutritious breakfast or snack option that has gained immense popularity. To make this delightful treat, start by mashing a small avocado in a bowl and adding fresh lemon juice, Kosher salt, and freshly ground black pepper for a burst of flavor. Toast a slice of whole grain bread to your desired level of crispiness. Spread the mashed avocado mixture generously onto the toast. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil on top for added richness. For an optional touch, you can garnish with a sprinkle of Maldon sea salt flakes or red pepper flakes. Indulge in the creamy and satisfying goodness of avocado toast, packed with healthy fats and wholesome ingredients.


Ingredients

1 small avocado
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon Kosher salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 slice whole grain bread, toasted
2 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
Maldon sea salt flakes or red pepper flakes for garnish (optional)

Pancakes

Pancakes, a beloved breakfast staple, are incredibly versatile and delicious. To make fluffy pancakes, combine all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, melted butter (or vegetable oil), and a large egg. Gradually pour the wet ingredients into the dry mixture, stirring until just combined. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat, brushing it with vegetable oil. Pour a scoop of batter onto the hot surface and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown. Serve the pancakes with an array of delectable toppings like butter, maple syrup, confectioners' sugar, honey, jams, preserves, sweetened whipped cream, or even chocolate syrup. Enjoy the fluffy goodness!


Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour, (spooned and leveled)
2 tablespoons sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
1 large egg
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
Assorted toppings, such as butter, maple syrup, confectioners' sugar, honey, jams, preserves, sweetened whipped cream, or chocolate syrup
